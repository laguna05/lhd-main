Gethenian Foods

Gethenian cuisine is very limited in comparison to what you might be used to here on earth. Due to the planet's cold, harsh climate there are no mammalian products like milk, butter, and cheese. Their high-protein, high-carb foods are eggs, fish, nuts and Hainish grains. But fret not, there are some delicious treats you might enjoy.

Breadapple

Breadapple is a staple of Gethenian cuisine. It is a sweet, dense, bread-like dessert with an essence of apples and walnuts. One thing to note about the recipe is that though recipes like this often use butter here on Terra, Gethenians resort to vegetable oils as butter is not readily available to them.

Above we have an example of what a Breadapple would look like.





Ingredients



  • 1/2 cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp of Cinnamon
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1/2 cup of Sugar
  • 1/2 cup of Oil
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 1/2 cups of All Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp of Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp of Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp of Salt
  • 2 cups of Chopped Apples
  • 2 cups of Chopped Walnuts
